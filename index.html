Methodology

Data source and scope

This prototype uses MDIC raw trade data for 2025 and MDIC lookup tables for countries and NCM products.

The European Union is treated as one trading partner. For partner rankings, the EU is grouped as one bloc and every non-EU country is counted individually.

Percentages show the EU share of Brazil’s total exports, imports and total trade. State-level percentages show the EU share of each state’s total exports, imports and total trade.

Product categories are displayed using HS4-level headings derived from Brazil’s official 8-digit NCM trade data. Values are current US dollars, FOB.
