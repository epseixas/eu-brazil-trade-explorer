Methodology

This prototype uses MDIC raw trade data for 2025 and lookup tables for countries and NCM products. EU trade is calculated by filtering partner countries corresponding to the 27 EU Member States in the MDIC country table and aggregating them into a single partner: the European Union.

Values are current US dollars, FOB. Product labels use HS4 groupings derived from the first four digits of the NCM code.

Prototype · Data source: MDIC / Comex Stat raw database · EU treated as one bloc
