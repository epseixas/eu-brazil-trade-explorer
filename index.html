Methodology

Data source and scope

This prototype uses MDIC raw trade data for 2025 and MDIC lookup tables for countries and NCM products. The data were filtered to the 27 EU Member States and aggregated into one partner: European Union.

Version 0.1 deliberately does not show individual EU Member States. The only geographic subdivision shown is Brazilian states.

Values are current US dollars, FOB. Product labels are HS4 groups derived from NCM codes.
